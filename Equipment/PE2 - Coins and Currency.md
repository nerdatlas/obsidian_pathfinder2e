# Coins and Currency

## Coin Values
| Coins               | CP    | SP   | GP    | PP      |
| ------------------- | ----- | ---- | ----- | ------- |
| Copper Piece (cp)   | 1     | 1/10 | 1/100 | 1/1,000 |
| Silver Piece (sp)   | 10    | 1    | 1/10  | 1/100   |
| Gold Piece (gp)     | 100   | 10   | 1     | 1/10    |
| Platinum Piece (pp) | 1,000 | 100  | 10    | 1       |

## Other Currency
- Art Objects
- Gems
- Raw Materials (such as those used for the [[PE2 - Craft|Craft]] activity)
