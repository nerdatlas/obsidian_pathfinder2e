# Cone of Cold
## Spell 5
#PE2Cold #PE2Evocation 
**Traditions** arcane, primal
**Cast** ◆◆ somatic, verbal
**Area** 60-foot cone
**Saving Throw** basic Reflex

-----
Icy cold rushes forth from your hands. You deal 12d6 cold damage to creatures in the area.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 2d6.
