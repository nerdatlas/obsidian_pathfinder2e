# Create Water
## Spell 1
#PE2Conjuration #PE2Water 
**Traditions** arcane, divine, primal
**Cast** ◆◆ somatic, verbal
**Range** 0 feet

-----
As you cup your hands, water begins to flow forth from them. You create 2 gallons of water. If no one drinks it, it evaporates after 1 day.
