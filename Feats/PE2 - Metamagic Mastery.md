# Metamagic Mastery
## Feat 20
#PE2Sorcerer
Altering your spells doesn’t take any longer than casting them normally. You can use metamagic single actions as free actions.

#PE2Wizard 
Altering your spells doesn’t take any longer than casting them normally. You can use metamagic single actions as free actions.
