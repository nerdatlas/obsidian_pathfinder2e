# Snare Crafting
## Feat 1
#PE2General #PE2Skill 

**Prerequisites** trained in [[PE2 - Crafting|Crafting]]

---
You can use the [[PE2 - Craft|Craft]] activity to create snares. When you select this feat, you add the formulas for four common snares to your formula book.
