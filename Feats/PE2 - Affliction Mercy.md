# Affliction Mercy
## Feat 12
#PE2Champion

**Prerequisites** [[PE2 - Mercy ◆]]

---
The divine grace that flows through you grants reprieve from an affliction. When you use [[PE2 - Mercy ◆]], you can instead attempt to counteract a curse, disease, or poison.
