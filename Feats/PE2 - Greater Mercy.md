# Greater Mercy
## Feat 8
#PE2Champion

**Prerequisites** [[PE2 - Mercy ◆]]

---
Your faith enhances your ability to remove conditions. When you use [[PE2 - Mercy ◆]], you can instead attempt to counteract the blinding, deafened, sickened, or slowed conditions.
