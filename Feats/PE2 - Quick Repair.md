# Quick Repair
## Feat 1
#PE2General #PE2Skill 

**Prerequisites** trained in [[PE2 - Crafting|Crafting]]

---
You take 1 minute to [[PE2 - Repair|Repair]] an item. If you’re a master in [[PE2 - Crafting|Crafting]], it takes 3 actions. If you’re legendary, it takes 1 action.
